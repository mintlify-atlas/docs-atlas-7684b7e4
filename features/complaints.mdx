# Complaints & Reports

The Yoneily platform provides a comprehensive complaint and report management system that allows customers to submit feedback about products and promotions, and enables venue managers to respond quickly and track resolution status.

## How It Works

The complaint system creates a two-way communication channel between customers and venue managers, ensuring that issues are documented, tracked, and resolved efficiently.

## Submitting a Complaint

### For Products

Customers can report issues with products they've purchased or browsed:

1. Navigate to the product page
2. Click the complaint/report button
3. Fill out the complaint form with:
   - Description of the issue
   - Product details (automatically captured)
   - Venue information (automatically captured)
   - Any additional comments
4. Submit the complaint

The system automatically records:
- Submission date
- Customer information (linked to your account)
- Product ID and venue ID
- Complaint status (initially set to "pending")

<Note>
  All complaints are timestamped and assigned a unique tracking ID for easy reference.
</Note>

### For Promotions

Similar to product complaints, you can also report issues with promotional campaigns:

1. View a promotion detail page
2. Select "Report Issue"
3. Provide details about the problem
4. Submit for review

The system tracks which promotion the complaint relates to and routes it to the appropriate venue manager.

## Managing Complaints (Venue Staff)

### Viewing Incoming Complaints

Venue managers and staff can access all complaints related to their location:

- **Dashboard View**: See all pending complaints (resp = 0)
- **Filtered by Venue**: Automatically shows only complaints for your venue
- **Sorted by Date**: Most recent complaints appear first
- **Paginated Results**: Browse through complaints 10 at a time

### Responding to Complaints

To respond to a customer complaint:

1. Click on a complaint to view full details
2. Review the customer's issue and product information
3. Write a response explaining:
   - Actions taken to address the issue
   - Resolution details
   - Any follow-up steps
4. Submit the response

When you submit a response:
- The original complaint status changes to "resolved" (status = 1)
- A new record is created with your response
- The customer is notified (when email notifications are enabled)
- Your response is linked to the original complaint

<Info>
  Responses are attributed to the staff member who submitted them, creating accountability and transparency.
</Info>

## Complaint Statuses

The system uses several status indicators to track complaint lifecycle:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **resp = 0** | Complaint pending, awaiting venue response |
| **resp = [user_id]** | Response submitted by venue staff member |
| **status = 0** | New complaint or response |
| **status = 1** | Complaint has been answered/closed |

## Access Control

The complaint system respects user permissions:

- **Customers**: Can submit complaints and view their own complaint history
- **Venue Staff**: Can view and respond to complaints for their assigned venue only
- **Administrators**: Can view and manage all complaints across all venues

<Note>
  Public access is allowed for complaint submission (no login required), making it easy for any customer to report issues.
</Note>

## Complaint Details Tracking

Each complaint record includes:

- **Customer Information**: Linked to registered user account
- **Venue/Location**: Which venue the complaint is about
- **Product or Promotion**: Specific item being reported
- **Submission Date**: When the complaint was filed
- **Description**: Customer's explanation of the issue
- **Response Thread**: All responses from venue staff
- **Resolution Status**: Current state of the complaint

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="For Customers">
    - Be specific and detailed in your complaint description
    - Include relevant information like purchase date or product condition
    - Check back for responses from venue managers
    - Follow up if the issue isn't resolved to your satisfaction
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="For Venue Staff">
    - Respond to complaints promptly (check dashboard daily)
    - Provide clear explanations of how you're addressing the issue
    - Take ownership of problems even if not directly your fault
    - Use complaints as feedback to improve products and service
    - Mark complaints as resolved only after taking action
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="For Administrators">
    - Monitor response times across all venues
    - Follow up on unresolved complaints older than 48 hours
    - Use complaint data to identify problem areas
    - Provide guidance to venues with high complaint volumes
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

Customers can access their complaint history to:
- See all complaints you've submitted
- Check response status
- Read venue responses
- Track resolution progress

The system maintains a complete audit trail of all complaint activity for transparency and accountability.
